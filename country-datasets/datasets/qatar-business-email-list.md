
# Qatar Business Email List Dataset

## Overview

The Qatar Business Email List dataset contains structured business contact records used for research, analytics, and marketing intelligence.

## Dataset Fields

First Name  
Last Name  
Company  
Job Title  
Industry  
Email  
Country  

## Sample Dataset

This repository includes a small sample dataset for research demonstration.

## Use Cases

- B2B market research  
- Data science experiments  
- Business intelligence analysis  
- CRM testing environments
